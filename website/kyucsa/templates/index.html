Kyambogo University Computing
Students Association (KYUCSA).

KYUCSA is a students Association which aims at helping university students learn various skills in Tech. These include but are not limited to mobile and web development skills , design thinking skills, Networking & System Adminstration, and leadership skills.

  Explore

Concept of KYUCSA

The best way to learn is through practice and exposure to various technologies. By engaging in such activities, members are able to gain development and networking skilling.

Why KYUCSA?

For students to learn practical skills, solve problems through technology and inspire them to be world class developers, product designers, cyber experts and changemakers.

Target audience

KYUCSA activities are targeted at University students and any individual who wants to learn or build their development skills as well as grow their knowledge concerning tech.

Opportunities KYUCSA provide students with

  • Grow their knowledge on developer technologies and more through peer to peer workshops and events.
  • Gain relevant industry experience by solving problems for local organizations with technology based solutions.
  • Showcase their prototypes and solutions to their local community and industry leaders.
  • Getting inspiration to become world-class developers and changemakers from sharing others' success stories.
